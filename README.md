# QT Training Camp

Quantitative trading is one of the most competitive fields to break into out of undergrad, especially with recruiting starting early. With that in mind, I’m treating the next few months as a focused training period.

I’ve always been drawn to math and making predictions, whether that was analyzing & projecting NBA player performance or competing in the AIME. At the same time, I grew up in competitive environments through sports and speech & debate, where improvement came from showing up every day and putting in deliberate work.

Because of that, I don’t think there’s a better way to give yourself a real shot at something difficult than going all in and approaching it with full focus. For me, that means treating this as if I'm training like an athlete in the off-season, where I will be committing to a consistent daily process, taking it one day at a time, and trying to get a little better each session.

This repository is where I track that process.

## Structure

* `daily-training-log/`
  Tracking daily work + reflections

* `projects/`
  Applying concepts through hands-on projects

* `interview-prep/`
  Focused prep for quant trading interviews
